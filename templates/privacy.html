Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects information you provide directly to us, including:

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and password credentials.

Expense Data: Any expenses you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you choose to enter.

Usage Information: We automatically collect information about how you interact with the Service, including pages viewed and features used.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

Provide, maintain, and improve the Service, including displaying your expense history and generating summaries.

Send you technical notices, updates, and administrative messages related to your account.

Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests.

Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues.

3. Data Storage

Your financial data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest.

Your password is hashed using secure cryptographic algorithms. We never store passwords in plain text.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may use third-party services for essential functions such as hosting, analytics, and security. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are prohibited from using your data for any purpose beyond providing services to us.

We do not display advertisements within the Service, nor do we share your data with advertising networks.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also export your expense data from your account settings.

To exercise these rights or if you have questions about how we handle your data, please contact us through the Service's support channels.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Service's support channels.
